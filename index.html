About Me

I am a computational neuroscientist specializing in the intersection of artificial intelligence, computer vision, and cognitive science. My research focuses on understanding how biological and artificial systems process and represent information, with applications in AI-driven product development and brain-inspired computing.

I hold an M.Sc. in Life Science Informatics (CGPA: 1.7/5.0 German scale) from the Bonn-Aachen International Center for Information Technology (b-it) and a B.Sc. (Honours) in Physics (CGPA: 8.35/10) from the University of Delhi. My interdisciplinary background enables me to bridge theoretical concepts from physics and neuroscience with practical applications in machine learning and data science.
